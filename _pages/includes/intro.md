I am pursuing a PhD degree in the School of Electrical Engineering, at Guangxi University, Nanning, China. My major research is around the future sustainable pathway for the energy sector. My main research interests include but are not limited to integrated energy systems, energy analysis, circular economy, life cycle assessment, and sustainable development. Recently, I have been working on the whole system analysis, dynamic optimization, and sustainable estimation of a circular economy and community-based virtual power plant (CE-cVPP). I eagerly anticipate the participation of professionals to engage in the exchange of knowledge and offer valuable suggestions.
